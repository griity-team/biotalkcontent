## 1. Brand Elements

### 1.1 Brand Logo
BioTalk's logotype is simple and friendly due to the handmade stroke that enhances the organic feeling. To retain and build on that recognition, the logo should always be reproduced in a consistent and clear way.

> **DO NOT modify the logo in any way.**

---

### 1.2 Minimum Clear Space

**Clear Space:**
The BioTalk logo should be kept away from competing graphics. It should be surrounded by enough empty space marked with 'x' — derived by bubble chat form — a space equal in size to the letter height on the logotype.

**Minimum Size:**
Under no circumstances should any version of the logo be reproduced smaller than the minimum defined size. Use the proper version to ensure the best readability.

- Minimum clear space: 40% of logo area
- Minimum reproduction: 20% of logo area

---

### 1.3 Logo Misuse

The logo must be displayed using the correct CMYK/RGB swatches. Brandmarks should not be altered, redrawn, or manipulated in any way.

**❌ Do NOT:**
- Rotate the logo
- Distort the logo
- Change the primary logo color
- Use the primary logo color on any complex pattern
- Change the typeface style
- Use the logo below the suggested minimum size
- Use colors that lack sufficient contrast for clear legibility
- Use dark background on dark color logotype
- Use light background on light color logotype

### 1.5 Logo Variations Color

The full color combination is mandatory for every logo appearance. Alternative color combinations apply to all logos when used on a colored background.

**Usage Rules:**
- Avoid using full-color logos on photographs unless the logo sits on a black or white area of the image
- Accepted variations: white logo on dark green background, dark logo on light/beige background, white logo on photography, white logo on black background

### 1.7 Typography

Typography is a fundamental element of the design system. It provides a uniform structure that reinforces brand identity throughout all applications.

| Role | Typeface | Usage |
|------|----------|-------|
| Headline | **Bianco Serif** | Product names (front packaging), headlines |
| Body Text | **Basis Grotesque Pro** | All supporting text, descriptions, claims |

**Body Text Weights Available:**
- Light
- Regular
- Bold

**Guideline:** When using typography with imagery, position type over areas of clear space, not over busy areas.

*Brand tagline example: "Made with natural ingredients, safe for your skin"*

- Acceptable colors: black, white, gray, or variant colors
- Do NOT use brand hues in icons
- Match icon version to corresponding background
- Do not adjust line size below 0.1 mm

### 1.9 Packaging Hierarchy

All packaging contents should be **center-aligned**. The BioTalk logo placement should always be center-aligned **above** the product name.

**Typeface rules for packaging front:**
- Product name → **Bianco Serif**
- All other information → **Basis Grotesque Pro**

#### Standard Hierarchy Order (Top to Bottom):
1. Primary Logo
2. Graphic Element (Ingredients color)
3. Product Name
4. Product Purpose
5. Primary Claim
6. Product Claim
7. Netto

#### Soap/Bar Hierarchy Order:
1. Primary Logo
2. Primary Claim
3. Product Name
4. Subcategory
5. Product Claim
6. Netto

## 2. Digital Applications

### 2.1 Instagram Look and Feel

**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1080 px

**Content tone & style observed:**
- Natural, clean photography with soft warm backgrounds
- Product lifestyle shots with ingredients
- Educational/informational carousel posts (skin tips, ingredient benefits)
- Indonesian language copy for local audience
- Mix of product-focused and skin education content
- Content pillars: product features, skin tips, myth vs fact, ingredient spotlights, testimonials, giveaways

### 2.2 Website Look and Feel

**Dimensions:** 1920 px (Web format)

**Website structure observed:**
- Navigation: Beranda, Produk, Tentang Kami, Tips, Best Sellers
- Hero section with full-width lifestyle imagery
- USP icons: Natural Ingredients, Friendly for Pregnant Woman, Clear Ingredients, All Skin Types
- Product category sections: Skincare, Natural Soap, Bodycare, Bundle Set
- Educational content section ("Kenali beragam permasalahan kulit dan solusinya")
- Product grid with category filter tabs (Face mask, Toner, Serum, Eye Care, Treatment)
- Testimonial section
- Dark green (#153B34) footer with newsletter signup
